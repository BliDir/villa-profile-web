About This Room

Versatile, Considered, Calm

Bedroom Two is designed for flexibility and focus. Two single beds can be configured as a king at your request — ideal for couples or a pair of close friends who each want their own space. The room sits at the quieter end of the villa, shielded from the pool, and catches morning light through its garden-facing window from first light to midday.

A dedicated work nook with a solid timber desk and ergonomic chair makes this one of the few rooms in the villa suited to those who need to get something done. Fast WiFi reaches every corner, and the desk lamp is warm enough for late-night reading without disturbing a sleeping partner.

The en-suite shower room is compact but beautifully finished in natural stone, with a generous rain-head and quality toiletries. A built-in wardrobe and full-length mirror complete a room that gives you everything you need without excess.
