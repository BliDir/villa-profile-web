About This Room

Your Private Sanctuary

The Master Suite is the villa's most spacious bedroom — a 45 m² retreat designed for complete rest. A hand-crafted king bed sits at the centre of the room, dressed in crisp Italian linen and surrounded by warm teak joinery and soft pendant lighting that shifts from morning brightness to amber evening warmth.

Sliding glass doors open directly onto your private terrace, where a pair of rattan chairs face the tropical garden. Completely shielded from the main pool area, it offers a level of quiet that is rare in villa stays — a place to linger over coffee at dawn or a nightcap long after the rest of the villa has gone to sleep.

The en-suite bathroom is finished in honed Javanese marble with a double vanity, deep soaking tub, and a frameless-glass rain shower. A full-length wardrobe runs the length of one wall, thoughtfully fitted with drawers, hanging space, and a hidden safe. Blackout curtains ensure that the room stays as dark as you need, for as long as you need.

An in-room espresso machine, a selection of teas, and a stocked minibar complete the picture — because the best mornings begin before you leave the room.
