About This Room

Small in Size, Generous in Light

Bedroom Three is the most intimate of the villa's bedrooms, and also its sunniest. A queen bed sits opposite a set of slatted timber shutters that frame the pool below — a view that rewards early risers with glittering morning light and rewards evening lingerers with the orange flicker of underwater pool lights in the dark.

The room is compact by design, giving it a warmth that larger rooms sometimes lack. Every surface has been chosen carefully — a timber headboard, hand-thrown ceramic lamps, and thick cotton curtains that diffuse the afternoon sun into something soft and golden.

The en-suite is neatly fitted with a rain shower, a stone basin, and a mirrored cabinet stocked with quality toiletries. A built-in wardrobe provides ample storage for a week-long stay, and the room is fully air-conditioned with individual temperature control.
